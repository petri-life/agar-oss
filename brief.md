**Product:** FocusFlow — a minimalist task manager

FocusFlow is a task app that shows you exactly one task at a time. No lists, no
boards, no projects — just the single next thing you committed to doing. You add
tasks by typing them in plain language; FocusFlow orders them for you and reveals
them one at a time. When you finish one, the next appears.

**What's new in this release:**

- Added an AI "auto-prioritize" mode that reorders your tasks based on deadlines
  and a learned model of which tasks you tend to defer.
- Introduced a Pro tier ($6/mo) that unlocks auto-prioritize, calendar sync, and
  unlimited task history. The free tier now caps history at 7 days.
- Removed the manual drag-to-reorder gesture in favor of auto-prioritize.

**Positioning:** "Stop managing your tasks. Just do the next one."
